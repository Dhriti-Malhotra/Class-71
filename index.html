Places You Must Visit

Prithvi Cafe

I had visited in December, 2018

Quaint little restaurant tucked inside a by-lane of Juhu. Blink and you may miss the mesmerising entrance to the cafe. So stay alert and look out for the black signboard that says "Prithvi Cafe" while you are on the main road. This restaurant has a good menu with various dishes and affordable prices. The food is delicious, the servings are quite large and some dishes are adequate for small groups of people. Attracts quite a trendy crowd of aspiring celebrities on show days. Be sure to book in advance in order to avoid disappointment. On other days, the crowd is quite hep and happening. Hosts the famous "Prithvi Theater Fest", which you must attend.

Reviews on some movies

Avengers Endgame
Excellent film to culminate MCU ride

I watched it today with my family and we all loved it. You should have some previous knowledge about the film in order to understand it completely. The movies I highly recommend are Infinity War, Civil War, Black Panther, Age of Ultron, Ant-man and the Wasp before watching Avengers Endgame. The entired movie pays homage to most of the other Marvel superhero movies. I believe that you will enjoy Avengers: Endgame a lot, even if superhero movies aren't your favourite, this movie is really good. There is at the beginning of the movie a (SPOILER ALERT) head dismemberment that is quite impressive. The movie is based on the most-talked action-packed Sci-fi series. The movie shows how the Avengers assemble once more in order to reverse Thanos' actions and restore balance to the universe. The movie is lengthy, and it does run for 3 hours (so I suggest some mini movie snacks), all worthwhile.

Amazing and humorous movie starring Jackie Chan

I have watched this movie so many times but it is really good so I want to keep watching it. The Spy Next Door is a really funny movie and it is definitely good if you want to relax and have a fun time. This is one of the best movies ever, one reason being because it starrs Jackie Chan (he is amazing and so are all his movies). Another reason why I like this movie so much is that it is a movie that has the perfect amount of action, perfect amount of humour, the right amount of romance and it has some children in it (and us kids are amazing). What I like about action movies that have some children as the main characters is that it makes me feel as though the movie is a safe movie for all ages to watch and is really funny and good for kids. I would probably recommend parental guidance for people under the age of 8. Otherwise this is a really good movie, I would definitely recommend it and I think that this is the best movie ever.
